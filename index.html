Revecast A connected system that helps services companies make better decisions about revenue, staffing, and hiring.

Platform Salesforce AI-Powered

Revecast connects the full planning and workforce lifecycle into one system. Instead of managing revenue forecasting, capacity planning, recruiting, and execution in separate tools, Revecast brings them together — so every decision is informed by what's actually happening across your business.

The Problem

Services companies don't have a clear, reliable view of what's actually going to close, whether they have the right team to deliver it, or when they need to hire.

Reactive hiringHiring too late once deals are already closing
Underutilized teamsPaying for resources with no billable work
Over-hiring earlyBringing on staff too soon, hurting margins
Missed revenueNot having the right team to deliver won work
Delivery riskProjects delayed due to staffing gaps
Inconsistent marginsUnpredictable financial performance

These aren't isolated issues — they're symptoms of the same underlying problem: a lack of alignment between pipeline, capacity, and hiring decisions.

Revecast PSA Revenue forecasting, capacity planning, project management, time tracking, and invoicing

The "plan and execute" side of Revecast. Forecast your pipeline, translate it into resource demand, identify capacity gaps, manage projects, track time, and invoice clients — all within Salesforce.

Revecast Recruiter Job management, candidate pipeline, AI resume processing, and interview management

The "hire" side of Revecast. When capacity gaps are identified, Recruiter manages the full hiring workflow — job postings, candidate matching, AI-powered resume screening, interviews, and offers. Includes Maya AI chat assistant, a public job board, and Agentforce-powered resume processing.

Revecast Forms Dynamic form builder for custom application experiences and data collection

A native Salesforce form builder used to customize the application experience on the job board, build intake forms, and collect structured data. Supports conditional logic, multi-object mapping, and guest user submissions.
