Revecast Product Integration Platform Embedded in Salesforce

What is Revecast Connect?

Revecast Connect is Revecast's integration platform, embedded directly in Salesforce. It connects your Salesforce org to external systems — HR platforms, accounting tools, project management systems, and more — so data flows automatically across your business without manual effort.

Connect is a core part of the Revecast system. It's the layer that ensures hiring decisions in Revecast Recruiter flow to HR for onboarding, project data in Revecast PSA syncs to financial systems, and your Salesforce data stays aligned with the tools your team already uses.

Key Capabilities

System-to-System Sync

Keep Salesforce data synchronized with external platforms. Configurable sync schedules, field mapping, and conflict resolution ensure your data stays consistent across systems.

HR & Onboarding

When a candidate is hired in Revecast Recruiter, their data can flow automatically to your HR platform for onboarding — no re-keying required.

Financial Systems

Sync project and invoicing data from Revecast PSA to your accounting platform so financial reporting stays up to date without manual exports.

AI-Assisted Integrations

Describe what you need in plain language and Revecast's AI agent builds the integration for you. No code, no engineering tickets.

How It Fits in the Revecast System

Connect is the bridge between the Revecast platform and everything else. Within the connected planning loop, Connect handles step 6 — syncing with HR and financial systems:

  • Recruiter → HR: New hires flow from the recruiting pipeline to your HR platform for onboarding
  • PSACore → Finance: Time entries, invoices, and project financials sync to your accounting system
  • External → Salesforce: Data from external tools flows back into Salesforce, keeping your CRM as the single source of truth
