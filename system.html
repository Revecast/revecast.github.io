How Recruiter Connects to Planning

Capacity gaps become jobs

When PSACore identifies that forecasted demand exceeds team capacity for a specific role, that gap becomes a job requisition in Recruiter.

Hiring data feeds back to planning

When a candidate is hired, their start date and role flow back to PSACore's capacity model, closing the gap and updating forecasts.

Time tracking validates the plan

Once the new hire starts delivering, PSACore tracks their actual utilization against what was planned, improving future forecasting accuracy.

Skills from recruiting inform staffing

Resume-parsed skills and interview feedback create a rich skills inventory that PSACore can use for future resource allocation decisions.
